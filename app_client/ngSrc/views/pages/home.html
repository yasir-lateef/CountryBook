Welcome to CountryBook!

CountryBook is a small web app that concentrates on gathering scattered information about the countries of the world and organizing it, so you don't have to Google for everything. Instead, you can just come over to CountryBook and get all the information you need, about the country you need.

CountryBook is a free and open source web application, developed with MEAN stack. Its JavaScript, all the way down. The source code has been uploaded to Github. If you have any suggestions, please feel free to contact me. Would love to hear from you.
